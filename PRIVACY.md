# Privacy Policy for Freezeframe

Freezeframe is built as a 100% local-first utility. Your data belongs to you, stays on your machine, and never leaves your local environment.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
* **Web History & Website Content:** Freezeframe reads the URLs and titles of your currently active browser tabs **only** when you explicitly choose to create a saved session capsule. 
* This data is processed entirely locally and stored securely using your browser's local storage sync engine.
* **No Tracking:** The extension does not monitor your browsing habits in the background, track your analytics, or log your keystrokes.

## 2. Data Transmission
* **Zero Server Architecture:** Freezeframe has no external databases, tracking servers, or telemetry APIs. 
* Absolutely no data is ever sold, shared, or transmitted over the network to third parties or to the developer.

## 3. Changes to This Policy
Any updates to this policy will be reflected directly in this repository.
